Verbal Description of Interface:
Our interface can handle a maximum of 6 documents at a time, including documents for training and ones for predicting. User may enter documents' names in the first column of input boxes, as well as characteristics, text filters and statistical methods for each document. There are checkboxes for each document to specify if a document is for training or for predicting. Once all the information are given, user may click on the TRAIN button to make a decision tree, and then click on the PREDICT button to get the results shown under the RESULTS column.
